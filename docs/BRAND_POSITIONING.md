# Brand Positioning

Last updated: 2026-05-21

## Source Of Truth

The main website is the official Sullivan County civic portal, not the tourism brand.

The homepage should answer one question quickly:

> What do you need to do today?

## Main County Site

Positioning:

> Sullivan County, Tennessee  
> Official services. Local government. Community information.

Supporting heritage line:

> Serving Sullivan County since 1779.

Primary job:

- Help residents get county business done.
- Make services, records, meetings, alerts, offices, and public information easy to find.
- Feel official, plain-language, searchable, mobile-first, accessible, fast, and trustworthy.

The brand promise:

> Find it fast. Get it done. Know where to go.

## Tourism Brand

Positioning:

> Where Tennessee Began. And Begins Again.

Primary job:

- Inspire visitors.
- Promote history, music, water, trails, race weekends, events, and trip planning.
- Own the more emotional visitor story.

## Homepage Hierarchy

1. Hero service search and top resident tasks.
2. County status, meetings, weather, alerts, closures, and emergency information.
3. Most-used county services.
4. Public notices and county updates.
5. Communities and local orientation.
6. Heritage as civic trust and identity.
7. Tourism bridge for visitors.
8. About county government.

## Copy Rules

- Lead with resident tasks before storytelling.
- Use plain verbs: pay, find, apply, request, report, contact.
- Keep tourism language below the civic utility layer.
- Use heritage to build trust, not to replace service navigation.
- Do not make the government homepage feel like a scenic photo gallery, campaign site, or tourism landing page.

## Current Homepage Copy

Hero heading:

> What do you need to do today?

Hero support:

> Search for a county service, office, form, public record, meeting, or local resource.

Tourism bridge:

> Visiting Sullivan County?  
> Start your Tennessee story here.

Tourism CTA labels:

- Open tourism guide
- Plan a weekend
